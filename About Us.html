About the Authors


This website was created by two freshman students from Delaware, attending an undisclosed school. We created this website for the purpose of informing the general public about the important speeding problems present on Kirkwood Highway, and what can be done to attain traffic safety in our area.*

About DelDOT


DelDOT is the Delaware Department of Transportation, a government agency with the goal of making Delaware roadways more safe and reliable. DelDOT is an organization that would likely be concerned with the issues on Kirkwood Highway**.

About Delaware


Delaware is the first state, and the one that we hail from. It is also the second smallest state. Its small size, as well as position near large metropolises such as Philadelphia, New York City, and Washington D.C. makes it full of important travel routes, especially in the northern and most populous parts of Delaware.
