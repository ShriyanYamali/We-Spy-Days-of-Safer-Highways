We Spy Days of Safer Highways

Our Mission

Our mission is to advocate for the installation of a speed camera on Kirkwood Highway in Delaware, driven by our commitment to enhancing traffic safety. As two impassioned students, we strive to raise awareness and rally community support for this vital initiative. Through collaboration with local authorities, engaging educational campaigns, and active involvement in our community, we aim to reduce speeding violations, prevent accidents, and ultimately create a safer environment for all road users. Our dedication to promoting responsible driving behavior and safeguarding lives fuels our determination to see the implementation of this speed camera, ensuring the well-being and security of our community.

Speed Cameras have been used throughout
the country in many places. The use of
speed cameras in Delaware has been previously
implemented in certain areas, however, on
Kirkwood Highway speed cameras have a
chance to save lives.

As residents of Delaware our whole lives,
we have always had a keen interest in
Delaware's traffic safety. We started
this project back in September of 2023
and plan to continue working on traffic
safety in Delaware until we can insure
that our roads are safe.

Since 2020, traffic deaths have been slowly increasing with
2022 being the most traffic deaths Delaware has ever seen.
